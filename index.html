KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide end-to-end turnkey solutions?

Yes, we handle everything from architectural design, municipal approvals, construction, to luxury interior finishing. You just have to turn the key and move in.

Are your package prices fixed?

Our package prices are transparent and mostly fixed based on the specifications. However, any custom changes or upgrades in materials will be accommodated with clear revised costings.

What is the typical timeline for a residential project?

A standard residential construction project takes about 8-12 months from foundation to handover, depending on the scale and complexity of the luxury finishes involved.
